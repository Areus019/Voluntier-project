About Voluntier

Voluntier is a website that helps people find volunteer work that fits their interests. It also helps organizations — big or small — gather enough volunteers for their programs.

Our Aim

To encourage more people to take part in volunteerism by making it easier to discover, join, and track meaningful causes.

What we offer

  • A homepage that shows recent forums, events, and top contributors
  • A gallery of recently concluded programs
  • A profile page with contribution points and friend list
  • Forums to communicate and ask for advice
